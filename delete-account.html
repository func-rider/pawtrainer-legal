Paw Trainer

Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Paw Trainer account and the personal data associated with it. Paw Trainer is a dog-training app that uses an AI trainer (Milo), Supabase (authentication & database) and the Anthropic Claude API.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address you used to register, to:

pawtrainer.privacy@gmail.com

Subject: Delete my account. Please include the email address of the account you want removed.

We verify the request comes from the account owner and then permanently delete the account. You will receive a confirmation once it is done.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your dog profile(s) — name, breed, age, photo and behaviour issues
  • Your training plans and lesson progress
  • Your chat history with the AI trainer (Milo)
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

Data retention

Account data is deleted within 30 days of a verified request, including removal from routine encrypted backups. We do not retain personal data after deletion unless required by law. Aggregated, non-identifying analytics that cannot be linked back to you may be kept.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play → Subscriptions before requesting deletion.

Contact

Questions about deletion or privacy: pawtrainer.privacy@gmail.com
